The Challenge

Growing a social media presence today requires constant engagement, lead scoring, and personalized interactions. Doing this manually is impossible at scale, and generic "bots" often lead to account shadow-bans and low-quality engagement.

The Strategy

I engineered a sophisticated growth engine powered by n8n that monitors account activity in real-time. The system uses AI to analyze comment sentiment and lead quality, transitioning high-intent users into automated DM funnels that feel personal and human.

Key Features

  • • Unified dashboard for monitoring engagement across multiple platforms.
