Lead Generation

Meta Ads Lead Capture

Tech Stack

Meta Graph API n8n.io GoHighLevel Webhooks PostgreSQL

The Challenge

Managing high-volume Meta Ads leads often results in slow response times and data leakage. Manual entry into CRMs is prone to error and delays the sales process, significantly reducing conversion rates.

The Solution

I built an autonomous pipeline that captures leads in real-time directly from Meta's API. The system sanitizes the data, checks for duplicates, and instantly pushes high-intent leads into a customized CRM (GoHighLevel) while simultaneously notifying the sales team via Slack and Email.

Key Features

  • • Real-time Meta Lead Forms sync via server-less webhooks.
  • • Automated lead scoring and qualification based on custom business rules.
  • • Instant multi-channel notifications (WhatsApp/Slack/Email).
  • • Secure data logging and performance reporting for ROI tracking.
